Session Impersonation:
Install EditThisCookie extension in google chrome.
Now open any site dashboard like Gmail, FB, Semrush or any with login credentials.
Now click on extension and then click on export button to copy the session details.
Paste this details on a notepad and clos the window tab without logged out.
Now go to history and delete the history along with cookies also. 
Now again try to visit the site. This time you won’t be logged in. you have to enter the login details. Now close the tab of site.
Now copy the text from notepad which you had copied in step 4. Now open the extension and click on import.
Paste the session details in import box and click on green color right mark.
Now again try to visit the site. In my case, it is https://www.semrush.com/dashboard/. 
It will automatically open your dashboard without asking for login details.
